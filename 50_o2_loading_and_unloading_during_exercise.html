    How This Course Works

    Three phases per concept
    1
    Watch and Read

    Video and PDF load side by side. A timer runs — the Reflect phase does not unlock until you have spent enough time with the material. No skipping.

    2
    Teach It Back

    Explain the concept in your own words as if teaching a friend. You must type — pasting is disabled. Minimum 30 words required before submitting.

    3
    See How You Did

    Your explanation and your instructor's appear side by side. Check off what you included. Your next review date is saved automatically.

    Things that are intentional
    ⏱ The timer is real. You cannot advance until you have spent enough time on the video. Use the Extend button if you need more time.
    ⌨️ No pasting. You have to type your explanation. Retrieval practice only works when you actually retrieve.
    📋 You grade yourself. Compare your explanation to your instructor's and check off what you covered honestly.
    🔁 Return visits are different. You'll see a welcome screen with your history and due date. Jump to recall or rewatch — your choice.
